**Q: How do you calculate win rate?**

A win = any called token that reaches 2x or more from its signal market cap within 24 hours.

Signal MC is recorded at the exact timestamp of our Telegram alert. Peak MC is the highest market cap reached in the 24 hours following the signal. We publish this data every Monday — nothing is cherry-picked.
